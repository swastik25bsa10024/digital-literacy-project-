## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

For this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The aim of the infographic is to help students understand the concept of digital literacy and its importance in daily academic life.

The infographic includes key topics such as the definition of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. I used simple icons, clear headings, and a clean layout to make the content easy to understand and visually appealing.

While creating the design, I found it interesting to use different visual elements to present information effectively. However, managing all the content within a single page without making it overcrowded was slightly challenging.

Overall, this task helped me improve my design skills and understand how to communicate information creatively using digital tools.
