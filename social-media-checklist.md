## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

### ✅ Do’s
- Use a professional profile name and picture  
- Share useful and positive content  
- Maintain privacy settings  
- Think before posting  
- Respect others online  

### ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal information publicly  
- Avoid posting inappropriate content  
- Do not engage in online arguments  
- Avoid spreading fake news  
- Do not misuse social media
