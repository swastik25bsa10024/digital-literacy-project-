## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone  
- Do not click on unknown links or messages  
- Always verify the sender before making any payment  
- Do not scan QR codes to receive money  
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication  
- Keep your apps and devices updated  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Regularly check your bank account statements  

### UPI Safety Tips
- Always check the payment request before approving  
- Use only trusted UPI apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm  

### Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
